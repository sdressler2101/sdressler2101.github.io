Our Services.

We offer a comprehensive range of wealth management services designed to help you build, preserve, and transfer your wealth according to your specific needs and goals.

We offer a comprehensive Investment Proposal service, encompassing portfolio construction, ongoing review, and strategic guidance tailored to your goals.

M&A is authorised to advise on and deal in the following products:

  • Deposit products
  • Superannuation, including self-managed superannuation funds (SMSFs)
  • Securities — shares and debentures
  • Managed investment schemes and managed funds
  • Government debentures, stocks and bonds

Our services are available at different levels to suit your needs, chosen investment strategy, and preferred method of implementation.

We provide comprehensive financial advice tailored to your individual circumstances, helping you navigate complex financial decisions with confidence.

Our rigorous investment selection process identifies opportunities across asset classes, drawing on deep market knowledge and independent research to build resilient portfolios.

We structure your wealth to maximise after-tax returns, incorporating superannuation, trusts, and other tax-effective vehicles to accelerate long-term wealth growth.
