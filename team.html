Our Team.

Our experienced team of wealth advisers are dedicated to providing personalised service and expert guidance to help you achieve your financial goals.

Tim Macphillamy

Company Director

Tim is a Certified Financial Planner (CFP) and Fellow of The Institute of Chartered Accountants in Australia (FCA), with more than 20 years' experience in Private Wealth Management.

Tim has broad experience in the financial services industry having co-founded the wealth advisory division of Investec Bank (Australia) Ltd, where he worked for more than 10 years. Prior to this time, Tim worked as a Partner for Norton Knight Macphillamy, Chartered Accountants and has also worked in senior operational and taxation capacities within the investment banking industry.

Tim's skills cover wealth structuring, asset allocation, philanthropy, estate planning and intergenerational wealth transfer. His clients include senior corporate executives, self-employed business executives, retirees, private foundations and family offices.

Lorraine Kavanagh

Senior Relationship Manager

Lorraine has over 10 years' experience in the financial services industry, with a wealth of experience in client services and advisory skills. Lorraine holds a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Finance & Economics, a Masters of Economics awarded from the University College, Dublin and a Graduate Diploma in Applied Finance. Lorraine's strong work ethic and client centric approach underscores her reliable, accurate and timely service to clients.

Zoe Green

Client Relations

Zoe attends to client queries and requests. She has over 20 years of experience in banking and financial services, and 4 years as a financial planning assistant in Sydney. Zoe understands the importance of building meaningful client relationships by providing exceptional service with great communication. She has completed a Diploma in Office Administration & Computer Applications.
